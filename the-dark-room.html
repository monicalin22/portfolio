WHAT: UI/UX Exploration and App Development

‍HOW: Research, Ideation, Storyboarding, Wireframing (Sketch), Prototyping (Figma)

‍WHEN: February to March 2020

The objective of this project was to explore and develop an effective user interface and user experience for a mobile application. A secondary objective was to brainstorm a physical object that can be paired with this app. “The Dark Room” is an app that allows film photos to be directly accessed via a mobile device. After getting photos developed at a shop, users can access their photos by entering in a code. Additionally, there is a film camera that can connect to the app via bluetooth for photo access. There are also editing tools, though minimal, to enhance images.
