PRODUCT DESIGNOTHER WORKABOUT

I'm Monica,
and I love using design to connect people.

But if you’ve made it this far, you probably already knew that. I am currently a rising senior studying Psychology and Computer Science at Columbia University. I’m looking for new grad full-time or summer 2021 opportunities.

Creativity and art, which first came into my life in the forms of writing and film photography, is something that I view as essential to my future happiness. Additionally, I love connecting with others, learning through research, and analyzing data. Since Product Design offers all of these things, it seems that I’ve found my perfect career path.

My resume is quite diverse. I have worked in film development, marketing, wealth management, and the non-profit space. I’ve even published a book for 100,000 Instagram followers. In the past, my interests moved quickly, but I feel that design has finally grounded me. In fact, when I’m in the flow while designing, I work for hours on end. I am incredibly lucky to have found something that I enjoy so much, it doesn’t even feel like work.

I’m passionate about designing for engagement and want to create experiences that are informative, intuitive, and fun. I believe that users informs products, which means that understanding them is the most important part of the design experience.
