About Me

I'm Monica, but if you've made it this far, you already knew that. Read below to learn more about me!

I’m currently a rising senior studying Psychology and Computer Science at Columbia University. Art, which first came into my life in the form of writing and film photography, is something that I view as vital to my happiness in the future. Additionally, I love using data, researching, and bouncing off of feedback. Lastly, I love interacting with people. All of this makes me sure that Product Design is the perfect career path for me.

My resume is quite diverse in that I have worked as a film development intern, marketed content for start-ups, inputted data into Excel sheets at a wealth management firm, started my own non-profit, and even published a book for 100,000 Instagram followers. Sometimes, things feel quite whimsical for me and my thoughts and interests move quickly, but I feel that product design has finally grounded me as it incorporates a little bit of all the things that I love.

I’m passionate about designing for engagement and believe that any designed experience should be informative, intuitive, and fun. I believe that the user informs products and feel that understanding them is the most important part of the design experience. Feel free toconnect with me on LinkedIn or view my resume.
