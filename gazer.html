Gazer

WHO: A team of 2, (including myself.)

‍WHAT: Entry for Netflix Creative Jam with Adobe XD. Placed 23rd out of 600+ teams (2,300+ students.)

‍WHEN: June 22 (11:00 AM) to June 24, 2020 (10:00 AM PST.)

‍HOW: Interviews, Competitive Research, Wireframing (Figma), Prototyping (Adobe XD)

For this competition, Adobe + Netflix tasked us with creating a third-party website that would bring people closer together when streaming movies. My partner and I decided to create a platform that would allow users to simulate in-person viewing experiences while also capitalizing on activities you can only do online. As such, our product, Gazer, (with a name inspired by the endless viewing of the galaxy, ) allows users to view content in revolutionary ways. Users can join global theatres and watch anything with anyone from anywhere in the world. They can also create private rooms to watch scheduled and spontaneous content with friends. There is also a section for recommendations that are tailored to what users want to learn, what they have been watching, and what their friends are watching. Lastly, users can see what their friends just watched and reacted to, as well as save and react to their own favorite moments that they can access later down the line. Gazer, then, functions as an all-in-one platform for users to stay thoroughly engaged with streaming content in a plethora of ways. With Gazer, the possibilities are endless!
